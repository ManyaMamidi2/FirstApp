Date

SEC filings with coverage until this date.

Revenue

Revenue is the net sales of iPhone, Mac, iPad, Services and other products of Apple as well as any deffered revenue.

Net Income

Net income is calculated as the gross income of a company with cost of goods, taxes, and interest, etc. deducted.

EPS

EPS is a good indicator of a company's profitability. It is caluclated as the value of earnings per outstanding share of common stock over some period of time.

Operating Income

Operating income is calculated as a company's profit once operating expenses are deducted. Operating expenses can be expenses such as wages, cost of goods, rent, etc.
